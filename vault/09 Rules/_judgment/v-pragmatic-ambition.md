# Value · Pragmatic ambition

**Statement:** Reach for the ceiling on a ladder you've actually built. Vision without a route is fantasy; a route without vision is bookkeeping — hold both, refuse either alone.

**Why it's a root:** It governs what gets pursued and what gets cut — the meta-decision that pairs every big aim with a walkable path and a checkable number. The value that lets you chase the product-scale idea while still demanding the ablation.

**Tells when it's being honored:** a moonshot framed with its first concrete step; "show me the number before I believe it."
**Tells when it's being violated:** ambition with no route (hype), or a route with no ambition (busywork that ships nothing that matters).

**Frameworks that embody it:** [[f-2080]], [[f-value-mechanism]], [[f-strategic-systems]]
**Rules that serve it:** forwardable-export sequence (fidelity/readability > resolution > size-via-split); token-cost-per-value for harness/judgment components; BD/M&A GO/NO-GO discipline — gate on value-prop fit, willing to kill (not just pursue)
