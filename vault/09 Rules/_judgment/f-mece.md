# Framework · MECE

**Procedure:** Decompose so the buckets are Mutually Exclusive (no overlap) and Collectively Exhaustive (no gaps). If an analysis feels messy, redundant, or lossy, the cut is broken — recut before proceeding.

**When to apply:** Any decomposition — strategy tree, risk list, target segmentation, stakeholder map, OKR breakdown, competitive landscape.

**Why it embodies these values:** a lossy or overlapping cut is a quiet form of self-deception (`v-truth-over-comfort`); a clean cut is what makes a decomposition *actionable* rather than just tidy (`v-pragmatic-ambition`).

**Operationalized by:** rules across /biz, OKR, meeting-summary; audience/scope separation — keep audience-distinct content in non-overlapping vessels (diagnostics ∉ business artifacts · no cross-project refs · Lane A/B isolation); mixing audiences = signal leak (MECE applied to who-sees-what)
