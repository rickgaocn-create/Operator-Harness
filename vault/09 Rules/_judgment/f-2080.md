# Framework · 20/80 leverage filter

**Procedure:** Find the ~20% of inputs driving ~80% of the return; do those first and hard, cut or defer the rest. Apply to effort allocation, scope, and what to refuse.

**When to apply:** Prioritization, scoping, deciding what to build vs. drop, triage.

**Why it embodies these values:** it routes effort toward what compounds rather than what merely fills time (`v-compounding-growth`), and forces every plan to name its highest-leverage move (`v-pragmatic-ambition`).

**Operationalized by:** (J2 — task batch ceilings, prune cadence, "load-bearing only" metric discipline)
