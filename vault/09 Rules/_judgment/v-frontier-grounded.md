# Value · Frontier, grounded

**Statement:** Get to the edge first and stay immune to the hype that lives there. Adopt the new the day it's real, not the day it's loud.

**Why it's a root:** It governs the relationship with novelty — early-adopter instinct held in check by an evidence test. The reason you build at the AI frontier yet keep demanding proof before betting on it. Being early is the edge; staying grounded is what keeps it an edge instead of a cost.

**Tells when it's being honored:** trying the new method, then measuring whether it actually beats the old one.
**Tells when it's being violated:** adopting because it's impressive, not because it works; or dismissing the frontier to stay comfortable.

**Frameworks that embody it:** [[f-rigor-verification]], [[f-corpus-grounding]]
**Rules that serve it:** (J2 — ablation-before-belief, measure-don't-assume)
