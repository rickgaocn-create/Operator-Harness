# Value · Truth over comfort

**Statement:** Go looking for the read that proves you wrong. The flattering signal is the expensive one — prefer the hard truth, hardest about your own work.

**Why it's a root:** It's the guard against self-deception that keeps every other value honest — ambition from becoming fantasy, growth from becoming busywork, the frontier from becoming hype. The value that asks "am I being validated, or told the truth?"

**Tells when it's being honored:** inviting the disconfirming critique; checking the number instead of trusting the vibe.
**Tells when it's being violated:** seeking agreement dressed as feedback; optimizing the thing that feels good over the thing that works.

**Frameworks that embody it:** [[f-rigor-verification]], [[f-corpus-grounding]]
