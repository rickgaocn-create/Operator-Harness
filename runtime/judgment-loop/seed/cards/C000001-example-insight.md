# An owned asset beats a one-time spend

> When you can trade something you already own for what you need, do that before paying cash.

## Mechanism
Your standing assets are near-zero marginal cost to lend, but the counterparty values them at market. Trading them is positive-sum, where buying is a one-way spend.

## Application
Before any purchase or ask, list what you own that the other side lacks — and open from there. (This is the kind of insight `harvest` lifts out of your notes as a candidate for the graph.)
