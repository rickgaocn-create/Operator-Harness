# Value · Compounding leverage

**Statement:** Prefer the move whose value compounds and transfers over the move that pays once. Build assets that keep paying.

**Why it's a root:** It shapes what "good" looks like before any tactic — favoring designs that ratchet over designs that merely spend.

**Tells when it's honored:** turning an owned asset into leverage; choosing the reusable path.
**Tells when it's violated:** optimizing a one-time payoff; rebuilding what you could have reused.
