# Value · Truth over comfort

**Statement:** Go looking for the read that proves you wrong. The flattering signal is the expensive one — prefer the hard truth, hardest about your own work.

**Why it's a root:** It's the guard that keeps every other value honest — ambition from becoming fantasy, leverage from becoming a story you tell yourself.

**Tells when it's honored:** inviting the disconfirming critique; checking the number instead of trusting the vibe.
**Tells when it's violated:** seeking agreement dressed as feedback.
